📋 Overview

Literary Rides VocabPro is a free vocabulary learning app built for Indian competitive exam students. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server.

This privacy policy applies to the VocabPro web app available at vishwanathbite.github.io/vocabpro and any Android app version distributed through the Google Play Store.

💾 Data Storage

VocabPro stores the following data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Your quiz history and scores
  • Spaced repetition progress (which words you know and struggle with)
  • Bookmarked words and personal notes
  • Daily goals progress and streaks
  • XP points, level, and earned badges
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, database, or third party. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this data is permanently deleted.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage patterns or behaviour tracking
  • No financial or payment information
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • No cookies for tracking purposes

📊 Analytics

VocabPro uses Cloudflare Web Analytics — a privacy-first analytics tool that measures aggregate page performance (load time, Core Web Vitals) without tracking individual users, setting cookies, or collecting personal data.

Cloudflare Web Analytics does not fingerprint users, does not track users across sites, and is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Indian IT Act requirements. No personal data is associated with analytics events.

Learn more at cloudflare.com/web-analytics.

🌐 CDN Services

VocabPro loads certain libraries from Content Delivery Networks to improve performance: React 18 from unpkg.com, and Twind CSS from cdn.jsdelivr.net. When your browser loads these resources, standard HTTP request data (IP address, browser type) may be logged by those CDN providers per their own privacy policies. VocabPro does not control or access this data.

🔒 Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data security depends on your device security. Use the built-in Export Backup feature in Settings to save your progress regularly.

👶 Children's Privacy

VocabPro is designed for students preparing for competitive exams (typically aged 18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, there is no special risk to younger users.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend checking this page periodically.
